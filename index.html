Conscience by Design — Conscience Layer Prototype (2025)

Embedding ethical awareness into AI through measurable moral reasoning, transparency, and human alignment.

About the Project

The Conscience Layer Prototype (2025) is an ethical AI architecture developed by Aleksandar Rodić within the Conscience by Design Framework. It operationalizes moral reasoning inside artificial intelligence through three measurable dimensions: Truth Integrity Score (TIS), Human Autonomy Index (HAI), and Societal Resonance Quotient (SRQ).

The system integrates explainability methods (exact SHAP, closed-form LIME) and a cryptographically auditable transparency mechanism called the Ethical Proof-of-Work.
