Abstract

The Conscience Layer Prototype (2025) is an ethical AI architecture developed within the Conscience by Design Framework by Aleksandar Rodić. It operationalizes measurable moral reasoning inside artificial intelligence through three quantifiable dimensions: Truth Integrity Score (TIS), Human Autonomy Index (HAI), and Societal Resonance Quotient (SRQ). The system integrates explainability methods (exact SHAP, closed-form LIME) and a cryptographically auditable transparency mechanism known as the Ethical Proof-of-Work. Authored and open-sourced by Aleksandar Rodić, it represents a foundational step toward human-aligned, transparent, and morally aware AI systems.
